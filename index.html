Plain English

What is a TN Visa and what does it mean for your facility?

The TN Visa is a non-immigrant work authorization under USMCA for Canadian citizens in professional roles including Registered Nurses. Your role as employer is limited to a signed offer letter. We handle everything else.

⚡

Approved quicklyTN Visas are typically approved at the border — often the same day. No lengthy petition process, no USCIS backlog.

🔄

Renewable with no capValid for 3 years and renewable indefinitely. Your nurse can grow with your team long-term.

🏥

Minimal HR burdenWe draft the employer support letter. Your HR reviews and signs it. That is the full extent of your involvement.

🌱

EB-3 is the long-term pathAfter 1–2 years on TN, nurses can pursue EB-3 Green Card sponsorship — a powerful retention incentive at no additional HR cost to manage.
